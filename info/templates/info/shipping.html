Shipping

We've carefully worked our shipping prices out in order to offer you the best prices for the amount of products you're buying. We recommend you to include as much products as you can in one delivery, it will cost you less money! The more you buy, the less you pay for shipping per item.

If you place an order before midday, we aim to process and dispatch it on the same day, if not, next working day. As orders are too large for a standard letterbox, someone needs to be present to receive the parcel. If your order is shipped by An Post, delivery will be attempted once, and if no one is in, they'll leave a note and it is your responsibility to collect it from the post office as instructed in the note.

Ireland and UK Shipping

All our orders are shipped with An Post, and once dispatched will take 1-2 working days to arrive into Republic of Ireland, and 2-4 working days into UK.

If you spend more than 60 euros on products from our online store, the delivery of your order will be free of charge.

Please note that we use third-party companies for shipping and delivery dates we provide are the ones they provide us. Once we've dispatched a package, it's on them to deliver it on time. We will provide you with assistance on delivery dates, address change and refunds in case the parcel doesn't arrive on time, gets lost or is damaged.

A parcel is considered lost by the couriers after 20 working days of the dispatched day. If that time has passed and it hasn't been shipped, we will send it again free of charge.

Even though it is not our duty to deliver parcels, we understand you want your order to arrive as soon as possible, so if it hasn't arrive after 10 working days, we will refund the cost of the shipping while we wait for it to arrive.

To find more information regarding returns and refunds, please see our refund policy.
